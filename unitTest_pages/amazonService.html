Product description

Tap to hit the lock tumbler points at just the right time. Hit three lock tumblers and the lock will open. Be careful not to miss the tumbler hit points or you'll have to start the tumbler sequence again.

Each lock has three tumblers to crack, and each tumbler cracked means more hits to get the next one open.
